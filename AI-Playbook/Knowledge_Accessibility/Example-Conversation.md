## User Question

I'm helping a client transition into a career in HVAC. Which training program should I recommend?

---

## AI Response

Based on the workforce guide, the recommended pathway depends on prior experience.

For individuals entering the field without experience:

• Complete EPA 608 certification
• Begin NATE Ready-to-Work
• Explore apprenticeship opportunities through IECRM
• Review financial assistance programs

Source:
Regional Workforce Guide
Career Pathways Handbook
