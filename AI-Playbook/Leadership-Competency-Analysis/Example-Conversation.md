## Example Request

Analyze the attached interview notes, executive biographies, and competency framework.

Identify:

- recurring leadership strengths
- competency themes
- development opportunities
- organizational risks
- recommendations

Summarize your findings in language appropriate for a CHRO or executive leadership team.

---

## Why this Works

Rather than asking AI to "summarize" information, the prompt assigns a professional role, provides a structured analytical process, defines the desired output, and clearly establishes where human expertise is required.
