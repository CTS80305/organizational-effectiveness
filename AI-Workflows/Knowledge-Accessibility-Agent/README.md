# AI Knowledge Accessibility Agent

## Business Challenge

Employees and workforce navigators often have access to hundreds of pages of policies, training materials, reference documents, and procedures, yet still struggle to find the right answer when they need it.

The challenge was not creating more content—it was making existing organizational knowledge accessible, searchable, and actionable at the point of need.

---

## Workflow

1. Inventory existing organizational knowledge
2. Organize documents into a structured knowledge base
3. Design prompts that guide users toward practical answers
4. Use AI to retrieve relevant information and summarize key guidance
5. Validate responses through human review and governance
6. Continuously refine prompts and source materials based on user feedback

---

## Tools and Methods

- Custom GPT
- ChatGPT
- Prompt Engineering
- Knowledge Management
- Learning & Development
- Organizational Effectiveness
- Human-Centered AI Design

---

## Outcome

Developed an AI-powered knowledge assistant that consolidated more than 250 pages of workforce guidance into a simple conversational experience. Instead of searching through multiple documents, users could ask natural language questions and receive contextual, actionable guidance.

The approach reduced time spent searching for information, improved consistency across workforce navigators, and allowed staff to spend more time coaching people rather than locating documents.

---

## What This Demonstrates

This project illustrates how AI can improve organizational performance by increasing knowledge accessibility rather than creating additional content.

The greatest opportunity for AI is helping people access the right information at the right moment so they can make better decisions and perform their work more effectively.

---

## Core Principle

Organizations rarely have an information problem.

They have a knowledge accessibility problem.

AI transforms information into guidance, enabling people to focus on judgment, coaching, and high-value work.
